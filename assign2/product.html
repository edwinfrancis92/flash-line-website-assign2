Destination

National City

Take a trip down to National City on the Flash-Line. Explore the wonderful safe and lush city that is under the protect of Supergirl and her friends. Known as one of the happiest most hopeful place to exists!

Star City

Feeling like living on the edge? Take a trip down to Star City, once a slum, now an upcoming city under the protection of the Green Arrow. Who knows, you might get to see a gang war break out while partying hard like Oliver Queen and maybe you'll get saved the a man in the green a hood.

Gotham City

The city of the Batman and Batwoman. If Star City doesn't cut it for you, take a trip down to Gotham, explore Arkham Asylum and it's resisdences and appricate the city's Gothic architecture.

Keystone City

Once suspended in animation, protected against the again effects of time, Keystone City has started to sprout again! Home to Kid Flash and The First Flash! Go hear to explore a city coming out of the 1800s.

Smallville

Home of the great Superman, go hear to enjoy the lush farm lands. Milk the Smallville Cows, get super milk. Take a hike. Who knows you might just see the greatest superhero of all time swhooping in to visit his mum!
